About The Game

EverBlast: Blast and Match is not your average puzzle game.
Smash through vibrant block clusters, unleash wild power-ups, and complete mind-bending challenges! With thousands of levels, clever puzzles, and daily events, EverBlast turns simple taps into satisfying explosions of fun. Perfect for quick breaks or hours of casual mayhem — the next blast is just one tap away.

Features

  • Tap to Blast

    Match 2+ tiles to create explosive chain reactions.

  • Mega Power-Ups

    Rockets, bombs, and lasers for unstoppable combos.

  • Strategic Levels

    Every puzzle needs the right plan, not just speed.

  • Daily Events

    Stay active and earn epic prizes.

  • Colorful Design

    Crisp animations, juicy sound FX, and satisfying bursts.

  • Play without Wi-Fi

    Enjoy the full game experience anytime— no internet needed.
